Introduction to Internet & Computers

Duration: 10-25 minutes

How the Internet Works

Watch this animation to understand how data travels across the internet, from your device to servers around the world.

Key Concepts

IP Addresses

Every device on the internet has a unique address, like a phone number, called an IP address.

Packets

Data is broken into small pieces called packets that travel independently across the network.

Protocols

Rules that govern how data is formatted and transmitted between devices.

Hands-On Practice

Try these commands in your own terminal to see how the internet works:

  • ping google.com - Test connection to a website
  • tracert google.com - See the path your data takes
  • ipconfig - View your network settings
